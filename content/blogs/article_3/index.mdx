# ⚡ Cheapest TVS EV Is Coming Soon!

**TVS** is set to introduce a **budget-friendly electric scooter** ahead of the 2025 festive season. This new model will cater to price-sensitive customers who want to go electric but are hesitant about the iQube's premium cost.

> 📖 _Written by: EVgyan Team_

---

## 🛵 TVS to Launch an Entry-Level Electric Scooter by Festive 2025

TVS plans to release an electric scooter priced between **₹90,000 to ₹1 lakh (ex-showroom)**. It will be positioned **below the best-selling iQube**, giving customers a more accessible way to transition to electric mobility — especially amid **rising inflation** and **shrinking subsidies**.

---

## 🔋 Key Features (Expected)

- **Battery**: Estimated 2.2kWh unit (like the iQube) or slightly smaller  
- **Motor**: Likely a **Bosch hub motor** for high efficiency  
- **Design**: Focused on **simplicity** and **minimalism** to cut costs

> This combination is expected to deliver a good balance of performance and affordability.

## 🔌 TVS Electric Portfolio at a Glance

The upcoming scooter will join the brand’s growing EV lineup:

- **TVS iQube** (standard and S variants)  
- **TVS X** (premium electric scooter)  

In **2024**, TVS sold over **1.27 lakh electric scooters**, reinforcing its position as a key player in India's EV space.

---

## 🎉 Why It Matters

This **low-cost TVS EV** could play a significant role in:

- Making **electric mobility more accessible**  
- Bringing **TVS reliability** and **engineering excellence** to the mass market  
- Accelerating **EV adoption during the festive season**

> With a strong brand and smart pricing, this could be a game-changer for first-time EV buyers.
