## Windsor EV Pro vs Mahindra BE.6e Base Model: The Real EV Showdown You’ve Been Waiting For

The MG Windsor EV Pro and Mahindra BE.6e Base Model are two heavyweights in the growing Indian EV market. Both offer impressive range, solid features, and a premium driving experience—but the real question is: **which one delivers more value for your hard-earned money?**

Let’s dive in, now that the Mahindra BE.6e Base Model is priced at **₹19.40 lakh** (with the 7.2 kW charger).

## 🔋 Battery and Range: More Miles or More Power?

### Windsor EV Pro
- 52.9 kWh battery
- ARAI-certified range: **449 km**
- Real-world range: ~**380–400 km**
- DC fast charging: **20–80% in ~50 mins**

### Mahindra BE.6e Base Model
- 59 kWh battery
- ARAI-certified range: **535 km** (MIDC: 542 km)
- DC fast charging: **20–80% in 20 mins** (175 kW charger)

**Verdict:**  
Mahindra BE.6e wins on paper with a longer range and faster charging. Windsor EV Pro still meets most practical needs.

---

## ⚡ Performance: City Comfort vs Highway Power

### Windsor EV Pro
- 136 hp, 200 Nm torque
- 4 drive modes: Eco, Eco+, Normal, Sport
- Comfortable for city commutes

### Mahindra BE.6e Base Model
- 231 hp, 380 Nm torque
- Rear-wheel drive
- 3 drive modes: Range, Everyday, Race
- Single-pedal driving for a sportier experience

**Verdict:**  
Mahindra BE.6e delivers more power and highway performance. Windsor is smooth and steady for everyday city driving.

---

## 🚙 Design: Practicality vs Presence

### Windsor EV Pro
- Compact and practical
- Flush door handles, split headlights
- Dimensions: 4295 x 1850 x 1677 mm  
- Wheelbase: 2700 mm  
- Boot: **579 litres**
- Ground clearance: **186 mm**

### Mahindra BE.6e Base Model
- Bold, aggressive SUV design
- Dimensions: 4371 x 1907 x 1627 mm  
- Wheelbase: 2775 mm  
- Boot: **455 litres** + **45-litre frunk**
- Ground clearance: **207 mm**, Battery GC: **218 mm**

**Verdict:**  
Mahindra wins for size and road presence. Windsor is ideal for urban practicality.

---

## 🛋️ Interior and Features: Tech and Comfort

### Windsor EV Pro
- 15.6-inch touchscreen
- Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
- Beige upholstery, ambient lighting
- 360-degree camera
- Premium feel, but touchscreen-heavy

### Mahindra BE.6e Base Model
- 12.3-inch touchscreen
- Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto
- 6 speakers, virtual engine sounds
- Connected car tech
- More user-friendly interface

**Verdict:**  
Windsor wins on features. Mahindra keeps things simpler and more intuitive.

---

## 🛡️ Safety: Who Keeps You Safe?

### Windsor EV Pro
- Level 2 ADAS
- Lane keep assist, adaptive cruise control
- Autonomous emergency braking
- 6 airbags, 360-degree camera, ESC

### Mahindra BE.6e Base Model
- 6 airbags, rear disc brakes
- Brake-by-wire, drowsiness detection
- No Level 2 ADAS

**Verdict:**  
Windsor EV Pro leads with more advanced ADAS safety tech.
