# ⚡ MG Comet EV Price Hiked in 2025 — Still Value for Money?

MG has increased prices for the **Comet EV** for the **second time in 2025**, but it's also adding new features to sweeten the deal. Despite the hikes, it remains one of the **most affordable electric cars** in India.

> 📖 _Written by: EVgyan Team_

---

## 🆕 Key Highlights

- 📈 Up to **₹35,700** price hike across variants  
- 💰 Executive model now starts at **₹7.35 lakh (ex-showroom)**  
- 🆕 New features: **Reverse camera**, **foldable ORVMs**, **leatherette seats**  
- 🔋 **Powertrain unchanged** — still 230 km MIDC range  
- ✅ Still a solid budget EV choice for city commutes

## 🕒 Throwback: Price Hike in March 2025

Earlier this year, in **March 2025**, MG had already revised pricing for several trims:

- **Excite & Excite FC**: ₹6,000 hike  
- **Exclusive variants**: ₹10,000 hike  
- **Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS)**: ₹27,000 hike  
- **Executive & Blackstorm**: No change at the time — until now.

MG has bundled several **feature enhancements** to justify the increase:

- 🔁 **Reverse parking camera**  
- 🪞 **Electrically foldable ORVMs**  
- 💺 **Leatherette seats** (on top trims)  
- 🔊 **Upgraded audio system** (4 speakers, up from 2)

> These upgrades enhance comfort and convenience, especially for city usage.

---

## ⚙️ Under the Hood: Still the Same

While features evolve, the **mechanical DNA** remains unchanged:

- 🔋 **Battery**: 17.3kWh lithium-ion  
- ⚡ **Motor**: 42hp, 110Nm torque  
- 🚗 **Range**: 230 km (MIDC) | ~150–190 km real-world  
- 🔌 **Charging**: 3.3kW standard / 7.4kW AC fast charging (~3.5 hours full charge)

> Perfect for urban runs, with ultra-low running cost and compact size.

---

## ✅ Verdict: Still a Smart Buy?

Yes. Even after **two price hikes in 2025**, the **MG Comet EV** holds its ground as a **value-packed city EV**. With prices ranging from **₹7.35 lakh to ₹9.84 lakh**, the Comet blends:

- Urban practicality  
- New creature comforts  
- Proven electric efficiency

For budget-conscious EV buyers in Indian metros, it remains a smart, sensible choice.
