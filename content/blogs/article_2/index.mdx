## 🚨 Full Article: Ola Roadster X (2.5kWh) vs Revolt RV1 2.2kWh

Hello people!  
Let's move on to the electric motorcycle battle that has everyone abuzz this spring season. There are two budget-conscious players competing for your hard-earned money: the tech-rich **Ola Roadster X** featuring its 2.5kWh pack and the versatile **Revolt RV1** boasting a 2.2kWh punch.

---

## ⚔️ The Contenders

### Ola Roadster X: A Technophile's Paradise

Ola's bringing the swagger game strong with the Roadster X. This base variant featuring a 2.5kWh battery is loaded to the gills with gadgets and gizmos every tech lover’s heart can be set a-flutter for. It's priced at ₹99,999 and may not be the most affordable, but it's hoping to capture hearts through its feature-rich strategy.

### Revolt RV1: The No-Nonsense Commuter

Revolt's selling the RV1 as _"India's First Electric Commuter Bike"_ – designed for daily riders who don't want to make a fuss, but simply want to get from A to B. It's priced at ₹89,999, which is a neat ₹10,000 less than the Ola.

---

## ⚙️ Showdown Performance

### Speed and Zip

- **Ola Roadster X**: Top speed of 105 km/h, 0-40 km/h in 3.4 seconds.  
- **Revolt RV1**: Top speed of 70 km/h, 0-40 km/h in 7–8 seconds.

### Range and Juice

- **Ola**: 140 km range (IDC), 2.5kWh battery, 3-year/50,000 km warranty.  
- **Revolt**: 100 km range, 2.2kWh battery (portable!), 5-year/75,000 km warranty.

### Charging

- **Ola**: Hypercharging - 0–80% in 6.2 hours.  
- **Revolt**: IP65 Fast Charger - 0–80% in under 3 hours.

---

## 🛵 Ride Feel and Comfort

### Suspension and Braking

- **Ola**: Telescopic front + twin rear shocks; drum brakes (front & rear).  
- **Revolt**: Dual disc brakes (240mm) + Combined Braking System (CBS).

### Tyres & Road Presence

- **Ola**: 18-inch wheels for decent presence.  
- **Revolt**: Wider tyres (90/80-17 front & 110/80-17 rear) = better grip.

---

## 🧠 Amazing Technology Features

### Ola Roadster X

- 4.3-inch color LCD screen  
- 3 ride modes (Eco, Normal, Sports)  
- Cruise control  
- Regenerative braking  
- Reverse mode  
- OTA updates  
- Companion app with energy insights

### Revolt RV1

- 6-inch LCD cluster  
- Reverse mode  
- Built-in charger/document storage box

---

## 🌟 Standout Features

**Ola Roadster X**

- Brake-by-Wire tech  
- Rear Single ABS

**Revolt RV1**

- 250kg payload capacity  
- Portable battery

---

## 💸 Money Matters

- **Revolt**: ₹10,000 cheaper. Running cost: approx. ₹200/month. 5-year ownership estimate: ₹96,990.  
- **Ola**: Higher initial cost, but still cheaper than petrol in the long run.

## ✅ Choose Ola Roadster X if:
- You're a tech-lover  
- You want faster acceleration & top speed  
- You love gadgets & app control  
- OTA updates matter to you  
- You want slightly more range

---

## ✅ Choose Revolt RV1 if:
- You're a practical commuter  
- You want to save upfront  
- You need better braking & safety  
- You love the portable battery  
- You carry loads or a pillion often

---

## 🔚 Final Word

Both bikes are making serious waves in the budget EV space but serve different audiences. **Ola** aims at the connected rider while **Revolt** focuses on everyday practicality.  
Whichever you choose, you're getting a cleaner, smarter ride that saves you money in the long run. And that’s a big win — for you, and for the planet!
